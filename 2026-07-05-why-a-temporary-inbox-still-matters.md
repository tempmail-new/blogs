## Why use a temporary inbox?

When a site asks for an email you do not fully trust, a disposable address can reduce noise fast. The FTC warns, "Scammers use email or text messages to trick you into giving them your personal and financial information."

People searching for `temporary email`, `tempmailso`, or even the typo `tem email` usually want the same thing: a quick inbox with less clutter. A simple [temporary email](https://tempmailso.com) keeps newsletters and test accounts off your main address.
